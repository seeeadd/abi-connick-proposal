No. Systems don't care where you learned design. We start with what you already do and upgrade it. If you can work with clients, you can install these systems. Most people in the challenge are self-taught.
Will this be too basic if I've been freelancing for years?
Day 1 might feel like a refresher, but most experienced designers tell me the Pricing Audit still surfaces gaps they hadn't noticed. Day 2 is where it gets real. The Client Experience Engine and Scope Lock are the pieces that save experienced freelancers the most time. If you've been freelancing for years and still don't have a repeatable process, this is built for you.
Is this going to be a hard sell for Strategic Designer OS at the end?
No. The challenge gives you real frameworks you can use on their own. If after three days you want the full structured course with templates, worksheets, and ongoing support, that's what Strategic Designer OS is for. But plenty of people do the challenge and never buy anything else, and they still get results. So come for the challenge. See how you feel.
What do I need to do to prepare?
Register and you'll get a prep email 48 hours before Day 1. It'll take 10 minutes and it means you hit the ground running on the first session. Other than that, just show up with a notebook.
What if I miss a day?
This runs live and the free tier is live only. If you miss a session, it's gone. VIP members get lifetime access to all three replays, so if your schedule is unpredictable, that's worth looking at. But honestly, the energy and the Q&A on the live days is where most people get the biggest breakthroughs. Move things around if you can and show up.
How much time does this take?
Each session is 60 to 75 minutes. You'll probably spend another 30 to 45 minutes working through the frameworks and building your own versions. So roughly two hours a day for three days. You can still do your client work.
